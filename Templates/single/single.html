Bold blooms that attract pollinators and thrive in tough conditions

Agastache x 'Meant to Bee Royal Raspberry' is a standout choice for gardeners looking to add vibrant color and life to their landscapes. With its strong, raspberry-scented blooms, this plant not only brightens up any garden from June through September but also attracts a plethora of pollinators, including bees and butterflies. It's also quite deer-tolerant, meaning it can withstand and defy spots while maintaining its great appearance, making it a superb option for those looking to conserve water.

As both beauty and ecological benefits, this hardy plant is also excellent for cover browsing. This means you can enjoy its vibrant presence without the worry of it becoming a deer snack for local wildlife. Planting 'Royal Raspberry' in your garden is a smart choice for both aesthetics and sustainability, as it also must adapt to its surrounding thriving as a local ecosystem.

Planting & Care ⌄
Plant in full sun to partial shade in well-drained soil. Water regularly during the first growing season to establish a deep root system. Once established, this plant is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance. Deadhead spent blooms to encourage continuous flowering throughout the season.
